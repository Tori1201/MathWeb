What is Calculus I?

Calculus I is the study of limits, derivatives, and integrals, which are essential tools for understanding how things change. It forms the basis for solving problems in science, engineering, economics, and more. This course helps you build a strong foundation for higher-level mathematics and applied fields.

Key Topics in Calculus I

  • Limits and Continuity: Explore how functions behave near certain points.
  • Derivatives and Applications: Learn how to measure rates of change and solve real-world problems.
  • Integration and Techniques: Understand the area under curves.
  • Fundamental Theorem of Calculus: Discover the link between derivatives and integrals.
